# Healthcare-Analytics
Primary Health Care (PHC) performance analysis focuses on evaluating how efficiently and effectively healthcare centers deliver services to patients

Project Overview

This project presents a complete healthcare analytics solution and MIS dashboard aimed at tracking and improving the performance of Primary Health Centers (PHCs) along with telemedicine operations.

It provides stakeholders with real-time insights into key areas such as physician productivity, patient flow, consultation efficiency, and adherence to healthcare KPIs.

The system is designed using a SaaS-based, web-enabled reporting approach, allowing centralized data processing and easy access through dynamic dashboards for different user groups.

🎯 Business Objectives
Track and evaluate operational and clinical KPIs across PHCs
Enhance transparency in doctor utilization and patient movement
Facilitate faster and more accurate reporting for administrators and evaluation teams
Minimize manual reporting through automated ETL pipelines
Ensure data consistency, audit readiness, and regulatory compliance

📊 Key Dashboards & Insights
The Power BI dashboards deliver insights across several key areas:

Doctor Performance

Number of consultations, efficiency levels, and workload allocation

Patient Analytics

Trends in patient registrations, consultation success rates, and appointment compliance

Operational Efficiency

Queue management analysis, patient throughput, and identification of operational bottlenecks

KPI Monitoring & Compliance

Performance tracking using predefined thresholds aligned with healthcare standards and regulations

🔄 ETL & Data Pipeline Overview
The solution follows a well-defined ETL pipeline:

Raw Healthcare Data
↓
Python-based ETL (Cleaning, Validation, Transformation)
↓
Structured Analytical Tables (Star/Snowflake Models)
↓
Power BI Data Model
↓
Interactive Dashboards and KPI Reports

Core ETL Capabilities:

Automated data ingestion and preprocessing
Data validation and anomaly detection
Preparation of standardized datasets for KPI reporting
